At Tod Inlet there used to be a cement works. In the park there is a lot of leftovers from this industry. Some of it is garbage and not very pleasing to the eye, but some of the leftovers are actually kind of pretty. When the cement structures are overgown it can look very cool. Overall I don't mind all of the stuff left over from the conrete works, but the polution in the inlet is not something that should be tolerated.
