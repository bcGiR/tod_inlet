This will be a memory for Danielle and I to share for ever. Be jealous everyone else. I love you Danielle.

Today Danielle and I went out to Tod Inlet on a day trip. We saw many cool things in our trip but it was just nice to be in nature. We have not really had a nice trip out to the forest like this since last summer. I took lots of pictures and so did Danielle and as such I will be updating this site with lots of pretty pictures and cool things to click on. This will serve as a practice for basic webpage layout.


These Cairns can be found in the stream all the way down to the inlet. They look especially cool and ancient with the moss that grows on them.

Being outdoors is spiritual food for Danielle and I. We need the outdoors to lift our spirits, to give our souls calm. It is great exercise as well; you don't realize how hard you are working until the end. Being out in nature together is very good for all aspects of the wellness wheel (or so I am told) and I can believe it since I still feel so good a day after being out there. Danielle and I will have to have many more adventures this summer. Come september, I will be ready for another round of school.
